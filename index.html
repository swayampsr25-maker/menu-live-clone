About Us

Menu Live

Menu Live is a software which can primarily be used by restaurants and just about every type of foodservice franchise. The platform is designed to automate virtually every business process involved in restaurant management.

It can effectively manage orders, tables, bills & reports. The software can be accessed by kiosks, mobiles, digital screens, ipads and laptops making tasks such as access reporting and analytics easy to use anytime.
